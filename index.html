General features

In WPTheme4.me, we apply minimalist design principles to keep things simple and focus only for elements that are essential to convey a message to a website visitor. Content comes first is our moto.

Clean, beautiful and minimalist Wordpress themes perfectly suits for architects, photographers, designers, illustrators, visual artists and art agencies.

Minimalist approach is applied not only to layout in frontend, but also to the content management in backend. Creating, managing your online portfolio is easier than ever and it is done in native Wordpress environment. You do not need to learn how to customize dozen theme settings. Functionality is the key. Just add a text and a visual content (iliustrations, architectural drawings, videos, photos) and the theme will take care of the rest.

Responsive layout is de-facto in today's website design. Your visual content, be it video, picture or photo, will be adjusted to each screen size. Did we mentioned that it works on all (last 3 versions) browsers? Your Wordpress site will look perfect on Internet Explorer, Chrome, Firefox and Safari browsers.
